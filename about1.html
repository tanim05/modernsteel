About Us

  • About Us

Modern Structural Steel Ltd. (MSSL) started its formal operation with most experienced and self recognized professionals with having more than 15 years of Quality Steel Construction Experience in the relevant fields.
We are pleased to introduce ourselves as one of the organizations doing complete solutions of design, manufacture, supply and installation of Modern color coated steel Roofing, Wall cladding and Pre-Engineered total Building Systems.
MSSL has installed all the fully automatic state of art machinery at its factory for producing best quality products with a current Capacity of 2000MT.
MSSL has ability to fabricate small, medium and large-scale orders for steel building products such as Industrial building: Ceramics, Garments, Knit Dyeing, Warehouses, spinning mills, Textile Mills Feed Mills, largest span Hanger shed, Food & Beverage, Furniture's shed and Multi-storied commercial buildings and many more.

Design Team


Modern Structural Steel Ltd (MSSL) has an experienced and professionally qualified Structural Design Team with excellent track record of handling tedious situation of difficult buildings to accommodate architectural features. They are doing with incremental weather conditions and seismic situations as per latest code of practice. We work closely with each client from concept to reality, design to construction and owner occupancy.

MSSL's structural design services are:


  • High Rise Structures: Steel composite structures, pre engineered/pre-fabricated building systems.

  • Industrial Structures: industrial plants, factories, power plants, etc.

  • Commercial Structures: ware houses, healthcare and educational buildings, bridge, theatres, stadium, exhibition hall etc.

  • Aviation/Aircraft Structures: Airport terminal, aircraft hanger, multipurpose complex etc.

  • Residential Structures: Houses, unit blocks, villas and more.

Software & Design Code


We emphasize more on quality in design. We have our own Software like STAAD- Pro, ETABS, TEKLA, SAP. RISA 3D V-8, RAM Connection V-8, Auto CAD-2018 and Quick Series, which are widely used in our design section.We always try to procure latest design manuals for our designers. We have latest Steel Construction Manuals: AISC Steel Construction manual Vol-13, MBMA Metal Building System Manual, Edition 2002, AISI Structural Welding Code -Steel, Edition: 2006.

The buildings are designed in accordance with the following codes:


A. MSSL applies the following codes for Load Calculation: and design of buildings:

  1. 1. BNBC-2015: Bangladesh National Building Code-2015(Bangladesh Gazette Notification dated 15.11.2017)

  2. 2. BNBC-1993: Bangladesh National Building Code 1993 (Bangladesh Gazette Notification dated 15.11.2006)

  3. 3. MBMA-2005: Metal Building Manufacturers Metal Building Systems Manual 2005 Edition

  4. 4 IBC-2006 (International Building Code)

    5. B. Hot rolled and built-up Sections are designed in accordance with:

  5. 5. Steel Construction Manual (2005) Thirteenth Edition (Latest) American Institute of Steel Construction Inc. (AISC), ASD and LRFD

    6. C. Cold-formed members are designed in accordance with:

  6. 6. AISI: American Iron and Steel Institute

    7. D. Structural Welding Code - Steel

  7. 7. AWS: D1.1/D1.1 M: 2006 Approved by ANSI, Nov. 29, 2005:20th edition (Latest)

Detailing Team


MSSL has an experienced of detailing Engineers and checkers that use AutoCAD/Tekla Structure for drafting, modeling, designing and drawing Steel Structures in compliance with AISC and other relevant building codes. And preparing details shop drawings according to client's specifications and requirements.

Production Team


Production of pre-engineered steel structures is a process that requires heavy machinery and precision in managing throughout the production process. MSSL is equipped with modern machinery along with expert engineers and Forman who actualizes the detailing of the shop drawing for each member. It has a number of sections, like, Cutting & Fitting, Fabrication, Welding, Grinding, and Sand blasting / shot blasting, Painting, Cold Forming and Fiber Shop.

Quality Control Team


All structures are designed and manufactured strictly according to international standard specifications ensuring maximum safety and economy by making optimum use of quality raw materials and sheets.MSSL has a laboratory to check the raw materials to verify the standard of materials supplied as well as to ascertain the quality of manufactured steel structures at various stages.

  • All raw materials required for the product are as per international standard specification.

  • All dimensional & weight checking is carried out at factory.

  • Quality Manual is maintained as per required of ISO 9001:2015

  • All welders are tested &trained and are certified welders. Field tests like, Dye Penetrate test, Ultrasonic test etc. are done to ensure highest quality of welding.
